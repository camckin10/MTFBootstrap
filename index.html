About

My Token Friend is a podcast dedicated to minority women living in the Midwest. We talk about pop culture, share personal stories, as well as interview awesome WOC from our community.

The podcast group consists of four women of color: Tarkor, Nehwoen, Karen, and Chelsey. Two of us from the group are “transplants”, meaning that we are originally not from Minnesota, and two of us were born and raised in Minnesota.
